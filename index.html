How It Works

Get your store agent-ready in three steps. No middleware or API keys required for discovery.

1

Install & Activate

Install CognitiveCart and WooCommerce. The UCP manifest is served automatically at /.well-known/ucp—no configuration required.

2

Add Agentic Data

Add GEO metadata to products (UN/CEFACT fields) to improve your Retrieval Qualification Score. AI crawlers discover your catalog automatically.

3

Agents & Pulse

Optional: Connect Stripe for AI-facilitated checkout. Enable Pulse Concierge for behavioral intent detection and AI-generated nudges on your store.

Powerful Features for Agentic Commerce

Everything you need to make your WooCommerce store discoverable and transactable by AI agents.

Pulse Concierge

Behavioral intent detection and AI-generated nudges. Trigger groups and intent triggers (variant cycling, cart stall, coupon hover, and more) drive conversions.

Abilities API

Register store capabilities for AI agents: add-to-cart, get-checkout-url, create-checkout-session, list-products, get-cart-summary, apply coupon, and more.

UCP Manifest

Served at /.well-known/ucp—no configuration required. AI crawlers discover your store automatically.

Kill-Switch

Emergency toggle to suspend all autonomous agent activity. You stay in control of when AI can interact with your store.

Intelligence Dashboard

Real-time view: UCP manifest status, crawl health (AI bot visits), Pulse activity, Kill-Switch, and Retrieval Qualification Score.

GEO Suite & Discovery

Add Agentic Data (UN/CEFACT metadata) to products. JSON-LD schema and Retrieval Qualification Score (0–100) for AI discovery.
